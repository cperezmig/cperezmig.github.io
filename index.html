About me

Hi, I’m Carlos Pérez-Miguel. I received the MSc in Computer Science from the University of the Basque Contry UPV/EHU, Gipuzkoa, Spain in 2005. I am currently pursuing my PhD in Computer Science at the same university in the Inteligent Systems Group. My research interests include parallel and distributed systems and specially, peer-to-peer networks.
